My Top 3 Favorite Places I have Traveled to

By: Austin Butler

1: El Nido Island, Philippines

My Favorite Parts of the trip:

  • Snorkeling with sea turtles
  • Exploring the markets and trying local food
  • Watching the sunset on the beach

2: Jerusalem, Israel

My Favorite Parts of the trip:

  • Visiting the Wailing Wall
  • Going to Museums
  • Exploring the Markets and talking to locals
